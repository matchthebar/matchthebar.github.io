What is the Barcelona World Cup?

The Barcelona World Cup is a tournament created to share a love for sports and build fellowship.

The tournament will mirror the World Cup in Russia, on a football 7 model.

It's the perfect excuse to spend the day with your friends in the Barcelona sun, enjoying good company and good football.




The tournament

What: 32 teams of 8-10 players

When: Sunday, July 1 from 10am - 6pm

Where: University of Barcelona soccer fields on Avenida Diagonal


Why sign up?

Football: Guaranteed at least 4 matches of 2 * 6'

Extra activities: A fan zone, a penalty tournament, Teqball, games for small children and a painting workshop. There will be two food trucks so you can grab a bite to eat and enjoy the day.

Benefits: Receive a welcome pack with access to the field, personalized jerseys with the name and colors of the country, accreditations and gifts.
